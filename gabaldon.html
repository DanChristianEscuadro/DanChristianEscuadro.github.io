Gabaldon Falls

Gabaldon Falls is surrounded by green foliage and huge rock formation. The main attraction of this falls was the ice-cold rippling water from the ten feet high waterfalls. This waterfall is a short 3KM hike from the town of Gabaldon, Nueva Ecija. This fall is surrounded by huge rock formations and rippling ice-cold water that comes from the Sierra Madre mountains about it. The water is so cool, clear, and clean, it is actually potable.
