Discover More About Our Gastronomy

“Rice Granary of the Philippines” is what Nueva Ecija is known as. Located in the Central Luzon region of the Philippines, it is also the center of agricultural research and production in the Philippines.

Aside from being one of the top rice-producing provinces in the Philippines, Nueva Ecija has a lot more to offer in terms of historical destinations, ecotourism destinations, farm-tourism destinations, and of course, delicacies. Many people go on travel seeking adventure or for sightseeing the beauty of a particular place. But for some, traveling also means eating foods or delicacies that are distinct in a certain location.
